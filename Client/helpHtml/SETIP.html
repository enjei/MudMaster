SetIP


Format: /setip {ip address}

If Mud Master is unable to determine your IP address, or you start up MM
before you make your internet connection, you can use /setip to let MM know
what your IP address is. This is the address people will see when they have
a chat connection with you. Also the address returned with connection
requests (/requestconnets).

  • {ip address} Your machines IP address.
