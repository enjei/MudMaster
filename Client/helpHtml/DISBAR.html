DisableBarItem


Format: /disablebaritem {reference number}
Format: /disablebaritem {item name}

Disables a bar item. When a bar item is disabled you cannot see it on the
status bar.

  • {reference number} The number of the bar item to disable.
  • {item name} The name of the bar item to disable.
