Procedures are always prefaced with the @ symbol. Parameters passed to the
procedures are always enclosed in parens ( ). Do not place spaces in the
parameter list unless you want to actually pass a space to the procedure.
@InList(Spells, Arithon) with a space after the comma would look for the
text " Arithon".
