Baritemshift

Format: /baritemshift {start item} {end item} {number}

BarItemShift allows you to shift items on the statusbar to the left or right
without having to hand edit all the positions.

  • {start item} The number of the first item you want to shift.
  • {end item} The number of the last item you want to shift.
  • {number to shift} If you put a positive number here all the items will be
         shifted to the right that many character places. If you put a negative
         number, they will all be shifted left.

    /baritemshift 1 3 5
    This will shift the items 1 through 3 five places to the right.
