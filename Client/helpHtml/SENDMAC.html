SendMacro


Format: /sendmacro {chat name} {key name}
Format: /sendmacro {chat reference number} {key name}

SendMacro sends one of your macros to another chat user.

  • {chat name} The chat name of the person you want to send to.
  • {chat reference number} The number of the person you want to send to.
  • {key name} The name of the key you want to send.
