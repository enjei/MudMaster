Keyboard


Ctrl+X       Exit Mud Master
UpArrow      Scroll through the history list.
DownArrow    Scroll through the history list.  Also clears the input line
             while you are editing.
Alt+UpArrow  Scrollback Buffer

Scrollback Keys
---------------
UpArrow      Scroll a line back in the buffer.
DownArrow    Scroll a line forward in the buffer.
PageUp       Scroll 24 lines back in the buffer.
PageDn       Scroll 24 lines forward in the buffer.
Home         Top of the buffer.
End          Bottom of the buffer.
ESC          Exit scrollback.

Editing
-------
RightArrow   Move cursor right.
LeftArrow    Move cursor left.
Home         Move to the start of the line.
End          Move to the end of the line.
Insert       Toggle insert mode on/off.
DownArrow    Clear the input line.
Ctrl+C       Insert a break character.
Ctrl+]       Insert an escape character.
