EnableBaritem


Format: /Enablebaritem {reference number}
Format: /Enablebaritem {item name}

Enables a bar item. When a bar item is enabled it will reappear on the status
bar.

  •   {reference number} The number of the bar item to enable.
  •   {item name} The name of the bar item to enable.
